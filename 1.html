EVERY BILKENTLI IS INTERESTING

“We met at a mutual friend's birthday party. Even though it was our first encounter, things felt so comfortable around him. It didn't take any more than one casual conversation concerning our shared interest in under-appreciated films and a little bit of whiskey for us to realize how much we have in common. The cornerstone of this was love for the work of the Canadian director, Xavier Dolan. We have watched a multitude of Dolan's movies together and shared some great moments during those times. It's a remarkable feeling to have someone care for you enough to take a genuine interest in your likings. It's incredible how a director from Canada can connect two people from Bilkent.”
